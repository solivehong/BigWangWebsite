Our Team

We are a group of highly motivated and creative people, discovering and innovating together to make precision medicine a reality through the catalytic power of big genomic data, statistical genetics, and artificial intelligence.

Wallace (Minxian) Wang is a principal investigator at Beijing Institute of Genomics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China National Center for Bioinformation. His lab is interested in understanding the genetic basis for human cardiovascular diseases and reducing the population-level disease burden by developing and applying statistical and artificial intelligence models in genome-wide association studies, genetic risk prediction, and precision medicine. His lab has the ultimate goal of assisting in guiding precise disease prevention, leading to healthier lifestyles, and in the discovery of new therapeutic targets.
